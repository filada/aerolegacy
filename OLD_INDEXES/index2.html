The Experience

More Than
A Museum

Step inside a living, breathing piece of aviation history. Our interactive exhibits put you in the pilot's seat — rotate, explore, and hear the stories of flight.

  • Interactive 3D aircraft you can rotate & inspect
  • Authentic engine sounds and cockpit audio
  • Cockpit simulator with real controls
  • Guided tours with audio narration
  • Archival film and photography
